About

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science at the Graduate School of Information Science and Technology (情報科学研究科) at Osaka University (大阪大学), Japan. In particular I am member of the Software Engineering laboratory, led by Prof. Katsuro Inoue.

I love experimenting with, tampering and ultimately destroying binary programs. When not in front of a screen, I enjoy taking pictures, landscape ones in particular. These two passions, united as one, forged my love for rendering software. $>

Research Interest

I am interested in code obfuscation and analysis.

I am working on analysing and detecting similarities in compiled binaries, in particular among different architectures.
